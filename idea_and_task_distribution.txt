## **ORCA – ORF Recognition and Comparative Annotator**

Design a program that analyzes one or two nucleotide sequences to identify and annotate open reading frames (ORFs) across all six reading frames. 
Core functionality: 
- accept sequences via NCBI accession number or local FASTA file
- validate and clean the input
- scan all six reading frames to detect ORFs above a user-defined minimum length
- compute per-ORF statistics including GC content, protein length, and codon usage.
